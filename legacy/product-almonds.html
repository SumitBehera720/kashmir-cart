Heritage Story

The Badam Orchards of Pulwama

During the spring season, the valleys of Pulwama are covered in beautiful white and pink almond blossoms. These historic orchards have been tended to by the same local families for hundreds of years.

Kashmiri Mamra almonds represent only a tiny fraction of global almond production. Because they are not pasteurized or treated with chemicals, they retain their unique curved shape and natural oils. Hand-shelled individually using traditional wooden hammers, they represent the ultimate natural health snack of the royals.

About Kashmiri Mamra Badam
Our Kashmiri Mamra Badam is completely raw and un-pasteurized. While mass-produced almonds undergo chemical steam pasteurization to prolong shelf life (which strips out natural oils), our almonds are simply dried in shell under mountain breezes, ensuring you get the maximum density of vitamin E and omega fats.
