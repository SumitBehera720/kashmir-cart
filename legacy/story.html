Guardians of the Valley's Treasures

For over a century, our family has held a deep bond with the soil of Kashmir. What began as a small apothecary inside the old quarters of Srinagar has grown into a global palace boutique, dedicated to preserving and sharing the pure, organic treasures of the valley.

Our mission is simple: to keep the ancient traditions of Kashmiri agriculture and craftsmanship alive. By cutting out corporate middle channels, we work directly with local saffron farmers in Pampore, wild beekeepers in Gulmarg forests, and badam orchard growers in Pulwama. This direct partnership guarantees that our customers receive the highest grade products, while the families who cultivate them receive fair, premium wages.

Every jar of saffron, tin of Kahwa, and bowl of almonds we sell is GI certified, lab-tested, and prepared using clean, traditional shadow-drying and hand-sorting methods. When you purchase from Kashmir Heritage, you are not simply buying a product—you are welcoming a piece of Himalayan history into your home.
